Theory: Topics in Computer Science

Research Project

Learning Goals

  • We are learning to assess strategies and initiatives that promote environmental stewardship with respect to the use of computers.
  • We are learning to analyze ethical issues and propose strategies to encourage ethical practices related to the use of computers.
  • We are learning to analyze the impact of emerging technologies on society and the economy.
  • We are learning to investigate different areas of theoretical research & career opportunities in computer science.

Topic List

Choose an emerging area of computer technology. Consider the list below, or you may select a different topic for me to approve. Do some preliminary research to figure out what is of interest to you!

  • Artificial Intelligence & Robotics
  • Computational Biology & Bioinformatics
  • Cloud & Fog Computing
  • Computer-Assisted Education
  • Data Science & Machine Learning
  • Data Management & Visualization
  • Human-Computer Interaction
  • Internet of Things
  • Molecular Information Systems
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Security, Privacy & Digital Democracy
  • Virtual & Augmented Reality
  • Wireless and Sensor Systems

Project Requirements

You MUST be able to include the following information in your final product:

  1. Describe the theoretical computer science research that led to it.
  2. What are the impacts (+ and -) of the emerging technology on:
    • societies and cultures around the world
    • local and world economy
    • the environment (+ suggestions to lower the negative impacts)
    • human health (+ suggestions to lower the negative impacts)
  3. Discuss any ethical issues that could propagate from the development or implementation of this technology.
    • Propose strategies to encourage ethical practices related to the emerging technology based on the ethical issues you discussed above.
  4. Discuss new career opportunities that will exist when this emerging technology is integrated into society.
    • Post-secondary education requirements for these careers.
  5. What other fields of study are incorporated into the emerging technology?
    • How is this vital to how the technology works?

Format

  1. Option 1: Learning Resource (1800-2000 word count).
    • Create a booklet or website that teaches what you have learned in your research.
    • Must be shareable with classmates.
  2. Option 2: Video (10 min maximum).
    • Could be similar to a Ted talk, Explain Everything (whiteboard style), etc.
    • Should be some visual component (images or diagrams, but minimal text!!).
  3. Option 3: Interactive seminar, in groups of 2-3 (~25 min).
    • Find videos (~4 min), articles, and other resources to help classmates learn about the topic.
    • Ask questions and moderate the responses, but keep in mind that you are the experts!
    • Should be some visual component (images or diagrams, but minimal text!!).

Whichever format you choose, you must address all 5 subsections and include a bibliography (all articles used in your research, whether quoted or not).

Deliverables

To assist with managing deadlines, the following deliverables will be expected in one-week intervals:

  • Deliverable #1: Topic selection (with preliminary research) and project format chosen.
  • Deliverable #2: Research outline & bibliography complete.
  • Deliverable #3: Draft version of product submitted for review (if creating a video, you should have a script and visuals by this point!).
  • Deliverable #4: Final Product Due.
